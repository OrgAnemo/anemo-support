📱 About Anemo

Anemo helps you preserve and share your most precious memories through the power of your voice. Record, transcribe, and share your stories with the people you love.

🔒 Privacy & Data

Your recordings are stored securely on European servers. You remain in full control of your data at all times. To request data deletion, contact us at anemoapp@gmail.com.

💳 Subscriptions

To manage or cancel your subscription, go to your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Anemo.
