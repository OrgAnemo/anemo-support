Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Anemo ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Anemo app.

For any questions, contact us at: anemoapp@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

  • Audio recordings — voice recordings you create in the app
  • Transcriptions — text generated from your recordings
  • Account data — email address and password
  • Memory content — titles, tags, emotions, people and places you add
  • Usage data — app interactions for improving the service

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Transcribe your voice recordings automatically
  • Analyze content with AI to detect people, places, and emotions
  • Store and organize your memories securely
  • Enable sharing of memories within private groups
  • Generate personalized Audiobooks from your memories

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Storage & Security

Your data is stored on European servers (Supabase / AWS EU region). All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Access to your data is protected by row-level security — only you can access your own memories.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following sub-processors to provide the service:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (EU servers)
  • OpenAI / Whisper — audio transcription and AI analysis
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Apple App Store — app distribution and payments

Each sub-processor is bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at anemoapp@gmail.com.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app: Profile → Delete my account.

You can also send a deletion request to anemoapp@gmail.com and we will process it within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Anemo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
